  • Team Vaayushastra was established in the year 2012 to represent Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering in the SAE Aero Design Competition. Since that year, we have participated annually in the advanced class of the SAE Aero Design Competition, held in the United States, 9 times.

    We have been one of the most consistent teams by finishing in the worldwide top 10 rankings, 7 times. The team is formed by 30 undergraduate students from different departments of the college who have the aptitude and interest conducive to the competition.
